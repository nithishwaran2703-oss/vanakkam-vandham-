Frequently Asked

Still Have Questions?

How far in advance should I book?

+

We recommend booking at least 2–4 weeks in advance for birthdays and 1–3 months for weddings to ensure availability and allow time for custom design work.

Do you provide floral and balloon services?

+

Yes! We offer fresh floral arrangements, artificial flower setups, organic balloon garlands, entrance arches, and full venue transformation packages.

What areas do you serve?

+

We primarily serve Tamil Nadu — Chennai, Coimbatore, Madurai, Trichy, and surrounding areas. We also take destination event bookings across South India.

Can I customize the decoration theme?

+

Absolutely! Every event is fully bespoke. Share your Pinterest boards, color palettes, or just describe your dream — we'll bring it to life.
